What We Do and Why It Matters

Pueblo Leak Repair Pros is a specialist leak detection and repair service operating in Pueblo County and southern Colorado. One specialty: finding leaks and repairing them. No HVAC, no remodeling side business, leak detection and repair is the entire operation.

The specialization matters because leak detection requires specific equipment, trained operators, and a commitment to a protocol most general plumbing operations do not maintain. The standard is acoustic listening, thermal imaging, and tracer gas to locate failures before any wall, slab, or ground is opened. Every call starts with detection. Every detection job ends with a confirmed failure location before any demolition begins.

Why Pueblo County Specifically

Pueblo County's leak profile is specific and well-defined. The combination of Pueblo Water's 180 mg/L hard water with housing stock spanning from 1890s galvanized steel to 1980s copper to 1990s early PEX creates predictable, recurring leak patterns that a Pueblo-specialist operation understands before arriving at the property.

The mid-century Belmont, Lakeview, Country Club, and Sunset Park neighborhoods contain thousands of copper supply systems now in the 40 to 60 year pinhole failure window. The historic Bessemer, Mesa Junction, and Downtown Pueblo housing stock has galvanized and cast iron systems past their service life. The East Side and rural county properties sit on expansive bentonite clay that stresses slabs and buried pipes with each seasonal moisture cycle.

Our Service Standard

Detection before demolition. Acoustic listening equipment, thermal infrared cameras, electronic amplifiers, helium and nitrogen-hydrogen tracer gas, and acoustic correlators for underground work: the right method for the leak type, applied before any surface is disturbed. Licensed in Colorado through DORA. License on file. Permitted work is permitted.
